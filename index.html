Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our products and philosophy.

How is WYEN different from other premium protein companies?

Beyond our ingredient standards, we approach formulation from an outcome-first perspective. Every product begins with a physiological goal (e.g., "optimize post-exercise muscle protein synthesis"), and we work backward to clinically-supported ingredient combinations. We also practice Full Disclosure Formulating™—no hidden proprietary blends. Plus, our 72-hour freshness guarantee and NSF Certified for Sport® certification set us apart in quality and transparency.

Are your products suitable for competition athletes concerned about banned substances?

Absolutely. All WYEN products are NSF Certified for Sport®, meaning every batch is tested for over 270 banned substances by an independent third party. We provide batch-specific certificates of analysis upon request. Many professional and collegiate athletes choose WYEN specifically for this rigorous testing protocol.

I have digestive sensitivities to protein supplements. What makes WYEN different?

Our protein sources are selected and processed specifically for digestibility. Our whey undergoes additional microfiltration to remove lactose and fats (resulting in <0.5g lactose per serving). Our plant proteins are fermented to break down hard-to-digest compounds. We also include digestive enzymes (protease, lactase) in relevant formulations. Most customers with sensitivities report excellent tolerance, but we recommend starting with our trial size to assess individual response.

What is your sustainability approach?

We measure our impact across three areas: (1) Ingredient sourcing (regenerative farming partners, marine-safe algae sources), (2) Production (100% renewable energy at our facility, water reclamation systems), and (3) Packaging (infinitely recyclable glass, compostable pouches, minimal secondary packaging). Our Plant Elite formulation has 40% less water usage and 60% lower carbon footprint than conventional whey production.

How should I incorporate WYEN into my existing nutrition plan?

Our products are designed to complement, not replace, whole food nutrition. We recommend: Performance Isolate within 45 minutes post-training, Cognitive Fuel as a coffee alternative for focused work sessions, and Plant Elite as a meal complement when whole food protein is inconvenient. All membership plans include access to our nutrition team for personalized protocol development based on your specific goals and schedule.
